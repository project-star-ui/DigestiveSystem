The Mouth: Your Body's Food Processor

Let's explore how your amazing mouth starts the journey of digestion!

Mouth Anatomy

  • 1

    Teeth - Like little knives and crushers that cut and grind food

  • 2

    Tongue - The taste tester and food mover

  • 3

    Salivary Glands - Make spit to soften food

  • 4

    Lips & Cheeks - Help keep food inside while chewing

How Digestion Starts in Your Mouth

Step 1: Chewing

Your teeth break food into tiny pieces while saliva makes it soft and mushy.

Step 2: Mixing

Your tongue mixes the food with saliva which has special juices to start digestion.

Step 3: Swallowing

The mushy food (now called bolus) travels down to your stomach through the esophagus.

Fun Mouth Facts!

1

The average person produces about 1 liter of saliva each day!

2

Your tongue has about 10,000 taste buds that help you enjoy different flavors.

3

Teeth are the hardest part of your body - even harder than bones!

4

Chewing starts breaking down carbohydrates (starches and sugars) right away.
